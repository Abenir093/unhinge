Stem

The GAS (General Academic Strand) is a flexible choice if you're unsure about which specific career path to pursue but want a well-rounded education. It offers a mix of subjects from different fields, such as business, social sciences, humanities, and basic sciences. This strand allows you to explore various areas of interest, helping you discover your strengths and passions. Whether you're considering further studies in college or entering the workforce, GAS prepares you with essential skills like critical thinking, communication, and time management. If you're looking for a versatile option that keeps your career options open, the GAS strand provides a broad foundation for future success.
