Humms

The HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences) strand is a great choice if you're interested in fields like law, education, psychology, journalism, or social work. It focuses on subjects such as philosophy, literature, history, and sociology, helping you develop strong critical thinking, communication, and research skills. This strand prepares you for careers that involve working with people, understanding societal issues, and making a positive impact on communities. If you're passionate about the human experience and want to pursue a career that involves helping others, advocating for change, or exploring cultural and social dynamics, the HUMSS strand offers a solid foundation.
